ABOUT ME

I grew up in a middle-class family, with my mother as a professor and my father as a management consultant, both of whom are brilliant analytical thinkers. While I admired their problem-solving abilities, my creative intelligence always stood out. Since learning to hold a crayon, I've been drawing endlessly, evolving from simple scribbles to detailed sketches and applying my creativity to everyday solutions for my family.

My ambition is to turn this innate talent into a career. A design project with IKEA, one of my father's clients, gave me the push I needed when one of my children's wardrobe concepts was well-received. This experience solidified my passion for design.

In high school, I explored creative career paths, excelling in entrance exams for Fashion Design (with a national rank of 831) and Architecture. After thorough research and discussions with my parents, I chose Design as my path forward.
